The Boyz : Members Profile and Details

The Boyz (더보이즈) is a 11-member South Korean boy group under Cre.Ker Entertainment.
The Boyz debuted on December 6th, 2017.
The group consists of Sangyeon, Jacob, Younghoon, Hyunjae, Juyeon, Kevin, New, Q, Haknyeon, Sunwoo, and Eric.
Hwall departed from the group on October 22nd, 2019.
